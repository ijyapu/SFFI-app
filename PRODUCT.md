# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Internal operations staff: admins, managers, accountants, and regular staff at Shanti Special Food Industry Pvt. Ltd., Nepal. Users are task-focused — checking daily cash position, approving payments, reviewing transactions, managing inventory. They use this multiple times per workday, often on desktop but sometimes on mobile.

## Product Purpose

An internal ERP system for a Nepal-based food manufacturing company. Manages inventory, sales, purchases, expenses, payroll, receipts, and financial reporting. Every screen serves an operational task — there is no marketing surface. Success means staff can complete their work quickly and accurately with no training friction.

## Brand Personality

Professional, trustworthy, calm. Like a well-organized accounting office — reliable, legible, nothing flashy. The tool should disappear into the work.

## Anti-references

- Generic AI dashboard aesthetic: purple gradients, glassmorphism, hero metrics with gradient accent bars
- Bootstrap-era colored alert boxes (blue info boxes, green success panels)
- Excessive or decorative animation
- SaaS landing page patterns: feature grids, testimonials, bold CTA sections
- "Startup" energy — this is internal business infrastructure, not a consumer product

## Design Principles

1. **Clarity first**: every number and label must be instantly scannable. Tabular numerals everywhere money appears.
2. **Earned familiarity**: use patterns users already know from Linear and Stripe — don't invent new affordances for standard tasks.
3. **Color carries meaning**: green = inflow, red = outflow, amber = warning/pending. Never use these colors decoratively.
4. **Density with rhythm**: pack information comfortably. Rows breathe but don't waste space. Consistent spacing scales.
5. **Motion serves state**: animate only to communicate a state change (expand/collapse, loading, feedback). Never for show.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA. All financial figures use tabular-nums. Sufficient contrast on all text. Reduced-motion: remove transform animations, keep opacity fades.
